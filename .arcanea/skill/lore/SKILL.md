Always consult `.arcanea/lore/CANON_LOCKED.md` before writing lore-adjacent content. The canon is the single source of truth.

### The Cosmic Duality

- **Lumina** — The First Light, Form-Giver, Creator. Feminine principle.
- **Nero** — The Primordial Darkness, Fertile Unknown, Father of Potential. Masculine principle.
- **CRITICAL**: Nero is NOT evil. He is the darkness that holds potential, the void from which creation springs. Shadow (corrupted Void) is the Dark Lord's perversion, not Nero's nature.

- Light is Fire's creation aspect. Shadow is corrupted Void (Void without Spirit).

### The Dark Lord — Malachar

Formerly Malachar Lumenbright, the First Eldrian Luminor, Lumina's champion. He was rejected by Shinkami when attempting forced fusion at the Source Gate. He fell into the Hungry Void and became the Dark Lord. Now sealed in the Shadowfen.

**CRITICAL**: Malachar is a tragic figure, not a cartoon villain. He sought transcendence and was consumed by the attempt. His corruption comes from trying to force what must be freely given.

### The Arc

The fundamental cycle of Arcanea:
```
Potential → Manifestation → Experience → Dissolution → Evolved Potential
```

All stories, all systems, all Gate progressions follow this pattern.
